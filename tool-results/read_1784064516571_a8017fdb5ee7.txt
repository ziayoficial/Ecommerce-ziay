   195→  7749→### P0 — Must fix before any production traffic
   196→  7750→
   197→  7751→**P0-1 — System prompt sent as `role: 'assistant'` instead of `role: 'system'`** (`src/app/api/agents/[agentName]/route.ts:94-97`, `src/app/api/orchestrate/route.ts:59-62`, `src/app/api/ai-reply/route.ts:83-86`)
