   542→### Phase 7 — 4 new payment webhook routes
   543→Each follows the same structure (modeled after the hardened whatsapp webhook):

   545→2. `src/app/api/webhooks/wompi/route.ts` — POST: verify via `WompiAdapter.webhookVerify` (X-Events-Signature header), parse `{ event, data.transaction.{id,status,reference} }`, map PENDING/APPROVED/DECLINED/ERROR/REFUNDED → Order.paymentStatus.

   547→4. `src/app/api/webhooks/payu/route.ts` — POST: parse JSON or form-encoded body, verify via `PayUAdapter.webhookVerify` (MD5 with state_pol), map state_pol 4/7/6/5 → paid/pending/failed/refunded.

   549→- All 4 use `await req.text()` (NOT req.json()) so the adapter can compute HMAC over the raw bytes — critical for Stripe/MercadoPago whose signature is over the exact byte sequence.
